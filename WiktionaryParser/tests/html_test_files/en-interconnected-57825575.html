English[edit]

Adjective[edit]

interconnected (comparative more interconnected, superlative most interconnected)

  1. intertwined; connected at multiple points or levels

Derived terms[edit]

  • interconnectedness

Verb[edit]

interconnected

  1. simple past tense and past participle of interconnect
