Noun[edit]

organic chemistry (uncountable)

  1. (chemistry) The chemistry of carbon-containing compounds, especially those that occur naturally in living organisms.

Usage notes[edit]

Compounds such as the carbonates are also included in inorganic chemistry. Gray areas include silicones and carboranes.
