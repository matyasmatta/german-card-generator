English[edit]

Etymology[edit]

xanthene +‎ -ic

Adjective[edit]

xanthenic (not comparable)

  1. (organic chemistry) Pertaining to, or derived from, xanthene.

Translations[edit]

pertaining to, or derived from xanthene
  • Italian: xantenico (it)
